Short Film — Crime Thriller — In Production

Production Update

Principal photography on Onus Probandi has commenced. Shoot day one covered key interior and exterior locations across Queensland, bringing the script to life with the full cast and crew.

Shoot Day 1 — On Location

The first day of production was completed successfully, covering the interrogation sequence, detective office scenes, and exterior location work. The Queensland-set crime thriller is now in principal photography, realising the grounded procedural vision developed over the past months.

The production is shooting locally in Queensland with a local cast and crew, led by writer Chris Beadnell and producer–director Adrian Alback.

About the Project

Onus Probandi is a Queensland crime thriller short film exploring what happens when forensic certainty fails and the pressure to close a case overrides the truth. Written by Chris Beadnell, whose script has received multiple international awards, the film is fully self-funded, produced and directed by Adrian Alback of Webstreamedia.
