Selected Work

A selection of long-form and short-form projects produced across documentary, independent film, and commissioned content.

Feature Documentary

Title: Follow the Sun
Role: Producer / Director
Year: 2008
Duration: 45 minutes

An independently produced documentary about filmmaking in Sydney. Originally developed under the working title Atom Heart Mother, the film was later released as Follow the Sun, starring Helen Middlemass and Barry Otto. It follows the creative and logistical challenges faced by independent producers navigating the bureaucratic realities of City of Sydney Council regulations while trying to bring a feature project to life.

Short Film

Title: Cliff’s Edge
Role: Producer / Director
Year: 2023
Credits: Multiple international film awards

Produced on a budget of under $1,500 AUD in February 2023, Cliff’s Edge was written by award-winning writer Chris Beadnell and is based on a real-life event that occurred in a Sunshine Coast mountain park. The film went on to receive multiple international awards for direction, screenwriting, acting and editing.
