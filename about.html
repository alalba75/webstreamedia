About Webstreamedia

Webstreamedia is an independent film and television production company focused on character-driven storytelling across documentary, factual, and original formats.

Based in Brisbane and working nationally and internationally, we develop and produce projects from early concept through to delivery — collaborating with broadcasters, platforms, brands, and creative partners.

What we focus on

Story-first development

Strong concepts, clear tone, and practical formats designed to travel — from pitch materials and sizzles to scripts and series bibles.

Lean production

Efficient crews, real locations, and smart scheduling that protect the story while keeping projects on time and on budget.

High-end finish

Editorial, colour, sound, and delivery workflows that meet broadcast, streaming, and digital platform requirements.

Experience

Our work spans independent film, documentary, factual television, and branded content, with experience delivering for broadcasters, platforms, and commercial partners.

Projects have screened across television, streaming services, festivals, and digital platforms.

Working together

We collaborate closely with commissioners, creatives, and clients to ensure each project has a clear creative direction and a realistic path to delivery.
