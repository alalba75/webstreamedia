Feature Documentary

Role: Producer / Director
Year: 2008
Duration: 45 minutes

An independently made documentary film about filmmaking in Sydney. Originally titled Atom Heart Mother and later renamed Follow the Sun, starring Helen Middlemass and Barry Otto. The producers navigate daily bureaucratic challenges under City of Sydney Council regulations.

Short Film

Role: Producer / Director
Year: 2023
Credits: Multiple international film awards

Cliffs Edge was produced for under $1500 AUD in February 2023. Written by award-winning writer Chris Beadnell, it is based on a real-life event that occurred in a Sunshine Coast mountain park. The film received multiple awards for direction, screenwriting, acting, editing and more.
