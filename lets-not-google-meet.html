Let’s Not Google Meet — A Google Transparency Documentary

Let’s Not Google Meet is a feature documentary in development exploring Google transparency, platform accountability and digital governance in automated systems. The film investigates how algorithmic decision-making, escalation pathways and structured support systems affect small business visibility and institutional trust.

As a platform accountability film, the project examines how modern digital infrastructure balances scale, automation and human oversight. Positioned within the broader field of digital governance documentary filmmaking, the work evaluates corporate responsibility frameworks, dispute resolution mechanisms and public transparency in technology systems.

Development is currently underway, with research interviews, documentation review and stakeholder outreach in progress. Festival submissions are targeted for 2027.
