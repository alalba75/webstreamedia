Feature Documentary

Role: Producer / Director
Year: 2008
Duration: 45 minutes

An independently made documentary film about filmmaking in Sydney. “Atom Heart Mother” was renamed to “Follow the Sun”, starring Helen Middlemass and Barry Otto. The producers struggle each day to meet the bureaucratic challenges of City of Sydney Council regulations.

Short Film

Role: Producer / Director
Year: 2023
Credits: Multiple international film awards

“Cliff’s Edge” was made for under $1500 AUD in February 2023. Written by award-winning writer Chris Beadnell, it tells the story of a real-life event that happened in a Sunshine Coast mountain park. The film won multiple awards for direction, screenwriting, acting and editing.
