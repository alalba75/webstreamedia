Denis Lwowski

Role: Mike

Denis Lwowski is attached to Onus Probandi in a performer capacity.

Denis Lwowski is an emerging screen actor with a strong interest in film and television storytelling. He enjoys digging into character psychology and bringing grounded, believable performances to camera. With a natural, relaxed screen presence, Denis works comfortably across a range of tones, from subtle dramatic moments to lighter, character-driven scenes.

Originally a native German speaker who lives in Australia, Denis brings an international edge to his work and enjoys roles that feel honest, textured, and human. His latest supporting role was in the crime comedy short "Shrooms", which was nominated to enter the AACTA Student Short Film division. He is now very much honoured to be part of Adrian Alback's crime short film "Onus Probandi".

Denis is always sharpening his on-camera craft through self-tapes, scene work, and ongoing creative development. He thrives in collaborative environments and is drawn to projects that value nuance, realism, and strong storytelling.

Russ Gallagher

Role: Inspector Gerard McKay

Russ Gallagher is an experienced Australian actor, presenter and voice-over artist with credits across film, television, commercials and live performance. His recent film appearances include The Bluff, The Whydah, No Escape, Patched, and The Carmen Chronicles, following earlier roles in productions such as Bloody Hell, Myall Creek, Escape & Evasion, and Baz Luhrmann’s Elvis. Russ has also appeared in television and web productions including Reef Break, Harrow, Grace Beside Me, and The Tradie Show.

Alongside screen acting, Russ is widely recognised for his work in national television and digital campaigns for brands including Maurice Blackburn, Planet Fitness, Supercheap Auto, Queensland Health, Dementia Australia and Forge of Empires, frequently appearing as both on-screen presenter and voice-over artist.

Russ brings a grounded Australian presence to character roles and has additional training in stunt and wire work, improvisation and television presenting. His skill set also includes firearms handling, martial arts, horse riding and public speaking, making him a versatile performer across drama, action and commercial productions.
